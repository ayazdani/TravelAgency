Diamond Head Campground

The beachside campground at Diamond Head is one of the most popular places to stay in Crowdy Bay National Park. It has caravan sites, additional walk-in campsites and picnic amenities.

National Parks Kangaroo Picnic

Jervisbay Holiday Park

Jervis Bay Holiday Park is a well-maintained, unspoilt, privately owned park on a crystal clear waterway accessing Jervis Bay. A unique feature of the park is its own private boat ramp and floating Pontoon, a perfect spot to launch your boat, canoe or Kayak.

Pet Friendly Private Boat BBQ

West Head lookout

Enjoy incredible views from West Head lookout, regarded as one of Sydney's best in Ku-ring-gai Chase National Park. Photograph Pittwater and Barrenjoey Head, or take a short walk from here.
